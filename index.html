Villa Balboa

    Objective

    To fulfil consistent occupancy rates of this luxury villa without expensive advertising expenditures nor relying on booking agencies that work on a commission basis.

    Work

  • Implemented an effective Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM) keyword plan geared toward attracting American tourists. This plan culminated with the client ranking above competitors.
  • Helped elaborating flexible price schemes across booking platforms to increase occupancy rates.

    •

    Results

  • Increased occupancy by 14% in same quarter compared to last month; occupancy continues to rise.
