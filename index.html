About

As an outspoken advocate of cross-cultural understanding I’ve helped manage the #WhyMexico digital campaign at The Atlantic Council, redesign the Mozzila-funded improvement of Ahwaa.com (the largest LGBT+ digital community in the MENA region) and have directed and produced “El Parking Place del Deseo,” a theater play that was funded to tour internationally by Université La Sorbonne.

I’ve provided impactful assistance to a myriad of different startups. From negotiating wholesale deals for two Mexican Spas throughout China at the age of 17 and 19, to promoting Lyft, and consulting the marketing strategy for Ecocredit, a SaaS based in Mexico.

Previously, I’ve interned in Vector, a Stock Brokerage firm and Bond-Moroch Public Relations & Brand Strategy, a multi-award-winning PR and Marketing firm. I’ve also been a research assistant to emeritus researcher Dr. Ida Prampolini.

I’m vehemently passionate about the psychologically of communication, and as such am an ardent researcher in related areas.

I’m proficient in Spanish, English, French and Portuguese.
